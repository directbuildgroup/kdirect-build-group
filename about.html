Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your project

Do you serve my area in Texas?
Yes! We serve all of Texas including Dallas-Fort Worth, Austin, Houston, San Antonio, and surrounding communities.
Are you licensed and insured?
Absolutely. We are fully licensed in Texas (TICL #806211919) with $2M general liability insurance.
How long does a typical remodel take?
Kitchen remodels: 2-3 weeks. Bathroom remodels: 1-2 weeks. Decks & porches: 1-2 weeks. We provide timeline with every quote.
Do you offer free quotes?
Yes! We provide free, no-obligation quotes within 24 hours. Contact us to schedule your consultation.
What brands do you work with?
We partner with premium brands including Sherwin-Williams, Trex, Kohler, Mohawk, and James Hardie.
Do you handle permits?
Yes, we handle all necessary permits for your remodel. We ensure every project meets Texas building codes.
